Diet



             Consuming a nutritious diet over the course of life helps to avoid malnutrition in all its forms, as well as a number of non-communicable diseases and disorders. However, a change in eating preferences has resulted in increased development of processed foods, increasing urbanization, and changing lifestyles. People now consume more energy-high ingredients, carbohydrates, free sugars, and salt/sodium, and often people do not consume enough fruit, veggies, and other nutritional products like whole grains.
